Ming received the 2025 Next Generation Award from the Society for Neuroscience in honor of her efforts in science education and outreach.  The award will be used to support the Atlanta Chapter for the Society for Neuroscience and the Fong Lab's STEM outreach program for local blind and visually-impaired youth.
